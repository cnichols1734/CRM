How it works

Three ways to use it

Forward an email

Got a lead in your inbox? Forward the message to your address. We'll pull the name, phone, email, and notes from the signature and the body.

Snap a business card

From your phone's photo app, share the picture to "Origen Inbox". Up to five cards per email — we read each one and create the contact.

Send a vCard or CSV

Attach a .vcf from your phone or a .csv export from another tool. We'll dedupe against your existing contacts before saving.
