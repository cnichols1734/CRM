1 Format Your CSV

Required

first_name or last_name

Optional

email, phone, street_address, city, state, zip_code, notes, groups

2 Example Format

first_name last_name phone groups
John Smith 5125551234 Buyer - New Potential Client
Sarah Johnson 2145559876 Seller - Active Listing;A

3 Quick Tips

  • Phone numbers should be 10 digits (e.g., 5125551234)
  • Separate multiple groups with a semicolon (;)
  • Duplicates are automatically skipped (matching email or phone)
