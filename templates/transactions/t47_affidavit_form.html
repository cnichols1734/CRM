Document Overview

What this declaration covers

The T-47.1 Residential Real Property Declaration is used in lieu of a formal affidavit regarding the survey of the property. By signing this document, the declarant(s) affirm:

  • There have been no changes to the property boundaries since the survey was prepared
  • No improvements have been made that would affect the survey
  • There are no encroachments or boundary disputes known to the declarant
  • The property information provided is accurate to the best of their knowledge

Signatures Required: The seller(s) will sign this declaration through DocuSeal. All information entered above will be pre-filled on the document before sending for signature.
